Hello!

I am a research scientist and techno-commercial analyst working at the intersection of hydrogen, industrial decarbonisation, and energy-transition strategy. My expertise spans pilot-scale green hydrogen research at the Indian Institute of Science (IISc) Bangalore, energy trading and risk management (ETRM) consulting for a European energy major, and independent applied policy roadmap development.

During my doctoral work, I designed and executed experimental campaigns on a 10 kWth oxy-steam downdraft gasifier, co-inventing two granted Indian patents. My professional background includes nearly 3 years at Infosys managing End-of-Day (EOD) risk analytics, implementing Kafka-based digital migrations, and integrating new trading products. My independent applied advisory work focuses on modeling state-level green hydrogen trajectories and developing AI-enabled policy intelligence tools for project developers.

Key Areas of Focus: Green Hydrogen production pathways, Oxy-steam biomass gasification, Industrial decarbonisation, Market Risk Analytics, and Energy Policy Strategy.
